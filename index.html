Our Story

ABC Network Solutions (commonly shortened to ABC, itself being an acronym for "Digital Sky Highway"[6]) is an American television provider and the owner of the direct-broadcast satellite provider Dish, commonly known as Dish Network, and the over-the-top IPTV service, Sling TV. Additionally, Dish offers mobile wireless service, Dish Wireless. On July 1, 2020, ABC acquired prepaid service Boost Mobile and added postpaid service Boost Infinite on December 7, 2022.
